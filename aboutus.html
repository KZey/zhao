ABOUT US

instar Model Management is an independent boutique agency, focusing on the top tier of talent in China.

Combining over 10 years of management in the progressive Chinese market and first hand experience in the western European modeling industry, Instars' avant-garde management style is truly unique and personal.

Instar takes great pride in its professionalism and efficiency in developing and nurturing new talent. The models have graced the covers of such publications such as Elle (China and Hong Kong), Vogue, Cosmopolitan, Harper's Bazaar, Marie Claire and Officiel.

Understanding the importance of recurring bookings for success and evolvement, the Instar team prides itself on its deepening warm relationships with clients. DKNY, Versace, Dior, Moschino, Paul Smith, Fendi, Kenzo and Chanel are some of the clients that booked Instar Models.

Its growing reputation as a major player in the Asian market has served them well as clients now look to them for Runway production, taking the initial idea and turning it into an well organized, innovatively designed professional runway event. With a strong vision, efficient management and increasing international presence, Instar goes from strength to strength.
